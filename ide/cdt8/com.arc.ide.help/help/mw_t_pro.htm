Debugging: Profiling

You can view profiling information when your application is running in the debugger two ways:

  • By selecting Debugger > Profiling to open a Profiling window
  • By by right-clicking in selected windows

The information displayed and the tasks you can perform are different in each case.

Some types of profiling data are displayed only if your program is compiled to provide them. If you receive a message that your program has no statistical profiling data or a profiling timer is not present, check your project's Properties > C/C++ Build dialog or makefile to ensure you have specified profiling.

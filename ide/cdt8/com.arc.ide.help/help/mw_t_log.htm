Capturing a Debugger Command Log

You can use a Log window to capture a history of commands (including internal commands) executed by the debugger, with any command output. Exporting the contents of the log is often useful for technical support requests.

To open a Log window, select Debugger > Log when your application is running in the debugger.

To export a log, do the following:

  1. In the Log window, right-click and select Save to File from the pop-up menu.
  2. Choose a directory for the exported log file.
  3. Type a filename, and click Save.
4.25.2.1 Working with the Log Window
  • To show or hide line numbers in the log, click the button.
  • To clear the screen, click the Discard button.
  • To filter the log, type a regular expression in the Reg expr box and press enter.
Only lines that contain your regular expression are displayed.

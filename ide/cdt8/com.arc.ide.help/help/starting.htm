Starting Animation

Before you can start animation, you must display the Animation toolbar (see Displaying the Animation Toolbar).

  1. By default, animation steps source statements. To step machine instructions, click the Instruction Stepping Mode button on the Stepping toolbar.
  2. Click Animate step-into or Animate step-over on the Animation toolbar.

A counter keeps track of the number of steps executed. Click the counter to reset it.

4.6.2.1 Adjusting Animation Speed

Click the up or down arrow on the Animation toolbar to adjust animation speed up or down.

4.6.2.2 Stopping Animation

To stop animation at any time, click the Suspend button.

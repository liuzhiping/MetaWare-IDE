Configuring Statistical Profiling

You can use the controls at the top of the Profiling window to change the PC range, counter size, bytes-per-count, and interrupt-frequency controls, and then send the parameters to your application. For detailed explanations of the parameters, see the MetaWare Debugger User's Guide.
The application must be compiled with -pg for statistical profiling.

To set and send parameters:

  1. Choose Profiling interrupts from the drop-down list near the top of the window:
  1. Change a parameter, such as the PC range or INT ticks.
  2. Click Send.

Any parameters you specify remain local to the Profiling window until you click Send. When you click Send, the entire set of configuration data is transmitted to the application, and the debugger displays a summary of the settings you sent.

As you continue debugging, profiling is performed with the parameters you selected and sent.

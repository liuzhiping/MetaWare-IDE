Debugging with ARC EM Interrupts

The debugger automatically detects the ARC EM interrupt registers when you specify ARC EM 1.1 or greater as the target processor.

You can open the Register bank and Interrupt state windows in a Debug configuration by selecting Debugger > Hardware.

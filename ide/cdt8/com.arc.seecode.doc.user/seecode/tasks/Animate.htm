Animating

Animation mode is a feature of the debugger that repeatedly executes a stepping action or debugger command. Animation also allows you to control the speed at which the command or step is executed.

Animating a step action

You can animate a step action (Step into source, Step over source, Step into instruction, or Step over instruction) to view windows update as the debugger steps through your code.

To animate a step action:

  1. Click the Animate button on the main toolbar
  2. Click any one of the four step buttons: Src Into, Src Over, Instr Into, Instr Over

To adjust the animation speed click the '+' and '-' buttons. The black indicator shows the relative speed selected for animation. The red indicator shows the performance level based on the relative speed.

Animating a debugger command

To animate a command:

  1. Click the Animate button on the main toolbar
  2. Enter a debugger command-line command in the Command: field
  3. Press [Enter] to execute the command.
